About Me

I'm also a person!

Who is this guy anyway?

I am a creator and craftsman driven by unquenchable curiosity and a desire to create joy. I strive for excellence in every task, knowing there is always more to learn.

As a passionate game writer, published short fiction author, and former game journalist with a background in programming & scripting (seven years as a web developer), as well as art & design (both formal and informal training), I bridge the divide between creative and technical while speaking the language of both.

I've worn many hats on AAA, indie, and solo game projects, and I have a variety of writing and publication credits including video games, short stories, scripts, op-eds, reviews, interviews, copywriting, technical writing, and documentation.
